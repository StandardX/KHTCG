Basics
	Players agree to a starting HP amount prior to the match
	HP may not be increased beyond the initial amount in any way during a match
	When a turn ends and a player has 0HP, that player loses
	In the event of both players having 0HP when a turn ends, a new turn begins and each player has 1HP, this is known as "Critical Time"
	If a turn ends with both players having 0HP during Critical Time, the match is a tie
	Players begin the match with an amount of Power equal to their initial HP divided by 20 rounded up
	Before the first Draw Phase of a match, players may set a WC in their respective WC space
	Before the first Draw Phase of a match, players draw 5 cards
	The max hand size is 7 by default, if excess cards are drawn, the player must discard until their hand is at 7 exactly before the match continues
	The max hand size may be increased by card effects
	If a player has no cards left to draw, they instead gain 1 Power whenever they would draw a card
	If a card effect demands the player to discard but they have no cards in their hand, they instead lose 1 EP of their choice
	Turns are separated into Phases which proceed in this order: Draw Phase > Prep Phase > Battle Phase > Rest Phase > End Phase
	
Power & Elemental Power Breakdown
	Power is gained during the first Draw Phase of a match, it is equal to the max amount of HP players start with divided by 20 rounded up, i.e. 200 initial max HP is 10 Power
	Whenever Power is gained, it must immediately be converted into EP as desired by its owner
	There is no limit to the amount of Power which can be gained or held, same for EP
	Once Power has been converted into EP, it may not be converted back nor swapped for other EP types
	Power may be given to the other player when gained only if both players agree to this, but not EP
	Power and EP are both public information to both players
	
Draw Phase Breakdown
	Players draw 1 card each from their Main Deck
	A coin is flipped and the winner of the coinflip goes first for the rest of this turn, or both players can agree who goes first
	No cards may be placed
	No Commands may be activated
	No Attacks may be made
	This Phase ends after the players finish drawing cards and the coinflip is completed
	
Prep Phase Breakdown
	Players take turns performing actions
	Cards may be placed
	Commands may be activated
	No Attacks may be made
	This Phase ends when both players are satisfied or when no possible actions may be performed
	
Battle Phase Breakdown
	Players take turns performing actions
	No cards may be placed
	Commands may be activated
	Attacks may be made
	This Phase ends when no further Attacks may be made, even if Commands were still possible
	
Rest Phase Breakdown
	Player's take turns using IC from their hand
	No other actions may be performed
	This Phase ends when both players are satisfied or when no ICs can be played
	
End Phase
	Player HP is considered for determining the match outcome
	No actions may be performed
	Abilities do not activate unless they specify doing so during the End Phase
	This Phase ends when nothing else which requires the End Phase needs attention
	When this Phase ends, 1 turn is considered to have passed for the purposes effects concerned with turns
	
Main Deck
	A Main Deck has exactly 20 cards
	At least 1 AC must be present in the Main Deck
	No more than 3 copies of any card may be in the Main Deck
	The total combined Levels of cards in the Main Deck may not exceed 100
	The above only needs to be true when the match starts

Side Deck
	There is no limit to the max amount of cards in the Side Deck
	At least 1 WC must be present in the Side Deck
	No copies of any card may exist in the Side Deck
	The above only needs to be true when the match starts
	The player may peruse their Side Deck at any time
	
Field
	Each player has the following spaces on their side of the field:
		Front Row x6
		Back Row x6
		WC Space x1
		LC Space x1
		ROD x1
		Main Deck x1
		Side Deck x1
	
Realm Of Darkness
	Cards which are destroyed or discarded are placed in their respective owner's ROD
	Cards do not activate in any way from the ROD
	Players may peruse the RODs at any time
	
Avatar Cards
	These are added to the Main Deck
	No copy of any AC may be present in the Main Deck
	ACs are placed in the FR
	ACs may Attack once per turn by default
	ACs possess the following traits:
		Level
		Element x1 or 2
		Ability x2 (For ACs these activate any number of times when the proper EP is present)
		Command x1

Group Cards
	These are added to the Main Deck
	GCs are placed in the FR
	GCs may Attack once per turn by default
	GCs possess the following traits:
		Level
		Element x1
		Ability x1
		Command x1
		
Form Cards
	These are added to the Side Deck
	FCs are placed in the FR, atop an applicable card
	FCs may perform 2 actions per turn comprised of Attacks and Commands
	FCs possess the following traits:
		Level
		Element x1 or x2
		Ability x1 (like ACs, these activate any number of times when the proper EP is present)
		Command x2
		
Equipment Cards
	These are added to the Main Deck
	ECs are placed in the BR
	ECs must be directly below their wielder and if they aren't, they are destroyed
	ECs may be turned inactive by their owner during the Draw Phase only
	Inactive ECs may be returned to the hand during the End Phase only
	ECs possess the following traits
		Level
		Equip: Requirements for attaching to FR cards
		Boost: The bonus Levels (if any) granted to the wielder
		Bond: If the wielder's name starts with any of the words listed for Bond, the wielder gains +1 Level
		Ability x1
		
Reprisal Cards
	These are added to the Main Deck
	RCs are placed facedown in the BR
	RCs may only be flipped faceup by their owner if their Command would activate in response to an action
	RCs are destroyed after their Command is activated
	RCs possess the following traits:
		Level
		Command x1
		
World Cards
	These are added to the Side Deck
	1 WC is placed in the WC space on the field before the first Draw Phase if desired
	WCs may only be swapped for another if their unique conditions allow it and only during the End Phase
	WCs generate EP during the Draw Phase
	WCs possess the following traits:
		Level
		Element: FR cards which share at least 1 Element with their allied WC gain 1 Level, or 2 if both Elements match
		Route: this determines which WC may be swapped in
		Core: this determines how much EP is generated
		Order: this determines which cards receive a special bonus
		
Limit Cards
	These are added to the Side Deck
	1 LC may be placed facedown in the LC space on the field during the Prep Phase
	LCs must be turned faceup when their Break condition is met
	LCs remain faceup as long as their Break condition is met, once it is not, they are destroyed
	LCs possess the following traits:
		Level
		Element x1 or x2
		Break: this determines when the LC is activated and what bonuses it provides while active
		
Item Cards
	These are added to the Main Deck
	ICs may only be played during the Rest Phase
	ICs are played from the hand directly
	ICs are destroyed after their Command activates
	ICs possess the following traits:
		Level
		Command x1 (some ICs have an "OR" to allow for more optimal use)

Terminology
	HP: Health Points
	Heal: Restore HP, does not go over the initial max HP
	Damage: Remove HP, does not go below 0
	Drain: Remove HP from one player and give it to the other
	Power: Resource for increasing EP, must be converted to EP immediately when gained
	EP: Elemental Power, necessary for some Abilities
	Ability: Automatically activate when conditions are met
	Command: Typically may activate once, conditions may apply
	Discard: Send card(s) to the ROD from the hand
	Destroy: Send card(s) to the ROD from the field
	Place: Put a card in the spot it is allowed to go to on the field
	Set: A card which exists on the field outside of a deck or the ROD is considered to be "set"
	Foe: The player on the other side of the field
	Opposing: A foe's set cards are considered to be "opposing"
	Allied: Your set cards are considered to be "allied"
	Attack: Use a card's Level to deal damage to the foe's HP
	Attacker: A card performing an Attack is considered to be an "Attacker"
	Negate: Typically for Attacks, this means the action is canceled without any further effect
	Draw: To pick up from the Main Deck
	AC: Avatar Card
	GC: Group Card
	FC: Form Card
	EC: Equipment Card
	RC: Reprisal Card
	WC: World Card
	LC: Limit Card
	IC: Item Card
	ROD: Realm Of Darkness, discarded and destroyed cards go here
	FR: Front Row, AC/GC/FC go here when placed
	BR: Back Row, EC/RC go here when placed
	Faceup: A card with its details displayed openly is considered "faceup", faceup cards may be examined by either player at any time
	Facedown: A card with its details hidden is considered "facedown", facedown cards may be examined at any time by the player who placed them only
	Active: A card placed in the orientation matching its designated space on the field is considered "active", vertical for all cards except WCs/LCs
	Inactive: A card turned to the opposite of its usual orientation is considered "inactive. Inactive cards do not do anything except take up a space on the field unless otherwise stated in their details
	Wielder: An FR card with an attached EC is considered the "wielder" of the EC
